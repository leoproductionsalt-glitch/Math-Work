Algebra Practice (9th Grade Level)

Problem 1: Solve the system of equations:
\( 2x + 3y = 12 \)
\( 4x - y = 7 \)
Hint: Use substitution or elimination.
Problem 2: Solve the quadratic equation by factoring:
\( x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0 \)
Answer: \( x = 2 \) or \( x = 3 \)
Problem 3: Find the slope of the line passing through points (3, -1) and (7, 5).

Geometry Practice (9th Grade Level)

Problem 1: In triangle ABC, ∠A = 40° and ∠B = 70°. Find ∠C.
Problem 2: A right triangle has legs of length 6 cm and 8 cm. Find the hypotenuse using the Pythagorean theorem.
Hint: \( c = \sqrt{a^2 + b^2} \)
Problem 3: Find the distance between points (2, 3) and (5, 7) on the coordinate plane.

Logic & Reasoning Practice (9th Grade Level)

Problem 1: If all cats are mammals and some mammals are pets, can we conclude that some cats are pets? Explain your reasoning.
Problem 2: A bag contains 4 red marbles and 6 blue marbles. What is the probability of drawing a red marble?
Problem 3: In a logic puzzle: "All roses are flowers. Some flowers fade quickly." Does it follow that some roses fade quickly? Why or why not?
